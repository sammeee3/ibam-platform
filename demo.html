About IBAM

Transforming businesses into discipleship platforms worldwide since 2009

Our Mission

IBAM exists to multiply disciples through faith-driven business by training Christian entrepreneurs to use their businesses as platforms for Gospel advancement and community transformation.

We believe every business can be a ministry, every entrepreneur can be a disciple-maker, and every workplace can be a mission field.
