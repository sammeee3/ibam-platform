Real Impact Stories

PK

Peter Kiprotich

Dairy Farmer & Church Leader, Kenya

🐟 Loan: $800 • 🎣 Trained: 2022 • ⚡ Disciples: 7 new believers

"When IBAM gave me my first loan of $800, I bought three dairy cows and started selling milk to my neighbors. But the real transformation came during the training - I learned how to turn every customer interaction into an opportunity to share God's love. Today, my dairy business supports my family and funds our church's youth programs. Seven people have given their lives to Jesus through conversations that started over milk deliveries. I've also trained 12 other farmers in our community using IBAM principles."

Current Impact: Monthly income increased 340% • Employs 2 neighbors • Supports 15 families through dairy cooperative • Church attendance grew 45%
MS

Maria Santos

Textile Business, Guatemala

"Started with a $1,200 loan to buy a sewing machine. IBAM taught me to weave prayer and scripture into my daily work. Now I employ 8 women from our church, and we pray together each morning before starting production. Three customers have started attending our church after seeing how we conduct business with integrity."

🎯 Impact: 8 jobs created • 3 new disciples • 100% loan repayment
JM

Joseph Mensah

Auto Repair Shop, Ghana

"My mechanic shop was failing until IBAM showed me how to serve customers like Jesus would. I learned to pray with customers who shared their struggles while waiting for repairs. Word spread about the 'praying mechanic,' and now I'm booked solid. I've trained 5 other mechanics in biblical business principles."

🎯 Impact: Revenue up 180% • 5 mechanics trained • 2 house churches started
